Frequently Asked Questions

What is the placement success rate at NETWORKERS HOME?

NETWORKERS HOME has a strong track record of placement success, with over 90% of our CCIE graduates securing top positions in leading networking companies. Our dedicated placement cell works closely with students to ensure they are prepared for interviews and industry challenges.

What kind of companies hire NETWORKERS HOME graduates?

NETWORKERS HOME graduates are highly sought after by top-tier companies, including Cisco, Juniper, HP, IBM, Accenture, and many other global networking firms. Our alumni have also secured roles in large multinational corporations and data centers.

What is the duration of the CCIE course at NETWORKERS HOME?

The duration of the CCIE course varies based on the track you choose (Routing & Switching, Security, Data Center, etc.), but typically takes between 6 to 8 months. This includes hands-on lab training, theoretical lessons, and practice exams.

Are there prerequisites for enrolling in the CCIE course?

While prior networking experience is beneficial, you do not need to have a CCNA or CCNP certification to enroll in a CCIE course at NETWORKERS HOME. Our programs are designed to cover all necessary foundational knowledge before advancing to expert-level content.

Do I get hands-on lab access during my CCIE course?

Yes, you will have access to one of the largest CCIE labs in Asia, equipped with the latest networking devices and software. This access ensures you get real-world, hands-on experience, preparing you for the CCIE lab exam and your future career.

What type of certification will I receive upon completion?

After successfully passing the CCIE exam, you will receive a globally recognized Cisco Certified Internetwork Expert (CCIE) certification, which is considered one of the most prestigious certifications in the networking industry.

What kind of support is available if I face challenges during the course?

Our team of CCIE-certified instructors and support staff are available to assist you throughout the course. You can access mentorship, one-on-one doubt-clearing sessions, and technical support to ensure your success in the course and on the exam.
