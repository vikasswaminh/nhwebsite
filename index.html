Frequently Asked Questions

What is the duration of the AI course?

Our AI course is designed to be completed over 6 weeks, with flexible study hours to suit working professionals. You can pace yourself according to your schedule, but we recommend dedicating 10-15 hours per week for the best results.

Do I need prior experience in programming or AI to enroll?

No prior experience in AI is required. However, a basic understanding of programming (preferably in Python) is recommended. The course includes foundational lessons for beginners, making it suitable for anyone looking to start their AI journey.

What kind of certification will I receive after completing the course?

Upon successful completion of the course, you will receive a professional certification recognized by top industry players. This certification can boost your career prospects in AI-related roles and showcase your expertise to potential employers.

Are there any live sessions or is it completely self-paced?

The course includes a combination of live sessions with industry experts and self-paced learning materials. You will also have access to recorded sessions, so you can catch up at any time.

How do I enroll in the course and what is the payment process?

Enrolling is easy! Simply click on the "Enroll Now" button and fill in the required details. You can choose to make a one-time payment or opt for our flexible monthly payment plan. We also offer financial aid for eligible students.

What support is available if I need help during the course?

Our dedicated support team and course mentors are available to assist you throughout your learning journey. You can contact them via email, chat, or live office hours to get help with course material or technical issues.

Can I access the course materials after completing the course?

Yes, you will have lifetime access to all course materials and recorded sessions even after you complete the course. This allows you to revisit the content anytime you need to brush up on your skills.
