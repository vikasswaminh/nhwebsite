Cisco SD-WAN Course Curriculum

The Cisco SD-WAN course at Networkers Home is designed to equip you with the knowledge and skills required to master Software-Defined Networking. Below is a 4-week detailed curriculum designed to give you hands-on experience in real-world SD-WAN scenarios.

  • Week 1: Introduction to SD-WAN and Cisco SD-WAN Architecture
    • Introduction to Software-Defined Networking (SDN) and SD-WAN
    • Understanding traditional WAN vs. SD-WAN
    • Cisco SD-WAN architecture and components (vEdge, vSmart, vBond, vManage)
    • Deploying SD-WAN in cloud and hybrid environments
  • Week 2: Configuring and Deploying Cisco SD-WAN
    • Initial device configuration and onboarding for SD-WAN
    • Establishing and configuring control plane connectivity
    • Configuring transport networks, IPsec, and routing
    • Deploying centralized policies for traffic management and prioritization
  • Week 3: Security in Cisco SD-WAN
    • Implementing security policies and VPN configurations
    • Understanding segmentation with VRFs (Virtual Routing and Forwarding)
    • Configuring firewalls, secure internet gateways, and URL filtering
    • Monitoring and troubleshooting security events in SD-WAN
  • Week 4: SD-WAN Optimization and Advanced Troubleshooting
    • Optimizing SD-WAN traffic with QoS and application-aware routing
    • Managing failover and redundancy for high availability
    • Network performance monitoring using Cisco vManage tools
    • Advanced troubleshooting techniques for SD-WAN environments

Cisco SD-WAN Course Curriculum

Explore our SD-WAN curriculum, designed to provide hands-on experience and in-depth knowledge of modern SD-WAN solutions and networking technologies.

In this module, you'll explore the fundamental concepts of Cisco SD-WAN architecture and its benefits for enterprise networking.

  • SD-WAN Overview: Understanding SD-WAN architecture, features, and benefits.
  • Traditional WAN vs SD-WAN: Differences between traditional WAN and SD-WAN, focusing on cost, scalability, and flexibility.
  • Key Components: Learn about controllers, edge devices, and vManage.
  • SD-WAN Use Cases: Applications in hybrid cloud, remote access, and multi-branch networking.

This module covers the deployment of SD-WAN solutions, from setting up controllers to configuring edge devices.

  • Initial Setup: Step-by-step guide to deploying SD-WAN controllers and connecting edge devices.
  • SD-WAN vManage: Using vManage for centralized management and configuration of the SD-WAN environment.
  • Policy Configuration: Configuring policies for traffic prioritization, security, and routing.
  • Integration with Existing Networks: Techniques to seamlessly integrate SD-WAN into your existing infrastructure.

Learn how to optimize and control traffic flow using advanced SD-WAN features, including dynamic routing and quality of service (QoS).

  • Traffic Optimization: Techniques to optimize WAN traffic and bandwidth usage.
  • Dynamic Path Selection: Automating route selection based on real-time conditions.
  • Quality of Service (QoS): Configuring QoS policies to ensure performance for critical applications.
  • WAN Optimization: Implementing WAN acceleration techniques to enhance throughput and reduce latency.

Explore how to secure your SD-WAN deployment, ensuring that data remains protected and compliant with industry standards.

  • Encryption Techniques: Implementing encryption protocols like IPsec and SSL to secure traffic.
  • Security Policies: Configuring security policies to control access and protect sensitive data.
  • Firewall Integration: Incorporating firewalls within the SD-WAN environment to defend against threats.
  • Secure Branch Connectivity: Best practices for securing branch offices and remote locations within the SD-WAN framework.
